ABOUT LAZER EFFECT

Lazer Effect is more than just a beanie brand — it’s a movement born from ambition, creativity, and style. Specialising in skull cap beanies, we craft pieces that are simple yet bold, versatile yet expressive. Whether it’s for the streets, the skate park, or everyday wear, our beanies are designed to be the finishing touch that makes your look stand out.

Built on authenticity and self-expression, Lazer Effect speaks to those who aren’t afraid to make their own statement. Each piece reflects the brand’s core values: originality, confidence, and a drive to push boundaries. What started as a small local project has quickly become a brand with big vision — redefining what it means to wear your individuality with pride.

OUR STORY

Founded by Trevor Gathiga straight out of high school, Lazer Effect began with nothing more than a dream and a few skull caps. Trevor saw an opportunity to create headwear that combined style with identity, and he turned his passion into a brand that resonates with young people everywhere.

Selling his first skullies locally, shaping Lazer Effect into a rising name in streetwear, Trevor’s journey is one of resilience and creativity. Today, the brand continues to grow with the same energy it started with: fearless, innovative, and dedicated to giving every customer more than just a product — but a lifestyle.
